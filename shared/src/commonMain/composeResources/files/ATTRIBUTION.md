# Offline map attribution

The bundled United States state boundaries in `us_states_20m.json` and `us_states_5m.json` are derived from the U.S. Census Bureau's 2025 1:20,000,000 and 1:5,000,000 Cartographic Boundary KML files:

https://www2.census.gov/geo/tiger/GENZ2025/kml/cb_2025_us_state_20m.zip
https://www2.census.gov/geo/tiger/GENZ2025/kml/cb_2025_us_state_5m.zip

The bundled highway vectors in `us_highways_2025.bin` are derived from the U.S. Census Bureau's 2025 state-based Primary and Secondary Roads shapefiles:

https://www2.census.gov/geo/tiger/TIGER2025/PRISECROADS/

The highway conversion retains only Interstate, U.S., and state routes (`RTTYP` I/U/S), creates three simplified display levels, quantizes coordinates to four decimal places, and is generated by `tools/generate_highway_vectors.py`. Source URLs, source SHA-256 digests, feature counts, and the generated binary digest are recorded in `us_highways_2025_manifest.json`.

Both sources are works of the United States government.
