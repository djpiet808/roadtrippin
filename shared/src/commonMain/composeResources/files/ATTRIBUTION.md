# Offline map attribution

The bundled United States state boundaries in `us_states_20m.json` are derived from the U.S. Census Bureau's 2025 1:20,000,000 Cartographic Boundary KML:

https://www2.census.gov/geo/tiger/GENZ2025/kml/cb_2025_us_state_20m.zip

The source is a work of the United States government. The conversion retains only the 50 states and District of Columbia, rounds coordinates to four decimal places, and is generated by `tools/generate_state_vectors.py`.
